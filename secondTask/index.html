Desde Peru con Amor

Peru is the third largest country in South America, twice the size of France!
The sights of Peru are known all over the world.
A wonderful feature of Peru is that there are
as many as three natural and climatic zones and a huge variety of landscapes, flora and fauna.
The plane in just an hour or two will take you from dry and deserted
the shores of the Pacific Ocean to the Amazon jungle or to the snowy peaks of the Andes.
